Director

Chairman

Ahmed Rashid Jibril

Ahmed Rashid Jibril is the Chairman for 748 Air Services Ltd. He has been actively involved in the operations and expansion of 748 Air Services Ltd, since its founding in 1994. As Managing Director, he developed and implemented all major strategic initiatives, which led to the growth of the company from one 748 Hawker aircraft to the current fleet of 11 aircraft made up of Dash 8-400, Dash8 -100 and Cessna caravans.

Ahmed is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accounts of England & Wales and the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Ontario, Canada. He is also a member of Institutes of Public Accountants of Kenya and founding member of the Association of Financial Analysts of Kenya.

He graduated at the University of Nairobi with a Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce. He is married to Farah Mohammed and blessed with four children.

Jeff is a pilot by profession with over 30 years aviation experience. He is a qualified simulator and ground school instructor.
